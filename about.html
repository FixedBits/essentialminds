Dr. Antonino Galitto

Dr. Antonino Galitto is a licensed clinical psychologist with over 15 years of experience specializing in cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT). He helps individuals navigate anxiety, depression, and life transitions by teaching practical tools for emotional regulation and thought restructuring. Antonino’s approach is structured, compassionate, and results-oriented—empowering clients to challenge unhelpful patterns and build lasting resilience. He also provides psychological assessments to support diagnostic clarity and personalized treatment planning. Known for his analytical mind and warm demeanor, Antonino creates a space where clients feel both understood and equipped to grow. His goal is to help people reclaim agency over their mental health and move forward with confidence.

Dr. Rachel Archibald

Dr. Rachel Archibald is a clinical psychologist specializing in family and parent support. She helps families improve communication, manage transitions, and strengthen emotional bonds. Rachel uses attachment theory and positive parenting strategies to guide families through challenges like divorce, sibling conflict, and behavioral concerns. She also supports parents of neurodivergent children and collaborates with educators to ensure consistent care. Rachel’s warm, empathetic style makes her a trusted resource for parents seeking guidance. Her goal is to help families create nurturing environments where every member feels seen, supported, and empowered to grow together.

Dr. Norma Williams

Dr. Norma Williams is a licensed psychologist focused on individual therapy and trauma recovery. She helps clients process emotional pain, rebuild self-esteem, and develop healthy coping strategies. Norma’s trauma-informed approach blends CBT, mindfulness, and expressive therapies to support healing. She works with individuals facing anxiety, depression, PTSD, and identity challenges. Known for her intuitive and affirming presence, Norma creates a safe space for growth and self-discovery. Her goal is to help clients rediscover joy, purpose, and resilience through compassionate, personalized care that honors each person’s unique journey.
